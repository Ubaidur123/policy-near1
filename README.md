<h1>Privacy Policy for NearbyCircle</h1>
<p class="updated">Last updated: July 2026</p>

<p>NearbyCircle ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mobile application designed for parents of children with autism, offering AAC tools and a community for connecting with other parents. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.</p>

<h2>Information We Collect</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Account information:</strong> email address and password, used to create and secure your account (via Firebase Authentication).</li>
  <li><strong>Profile information:</strong> your display name and your child's age, which you provide voluntarily to personalize your experience and connect with relevant community groups.</li>
  <li><strong>Community messages:</strong> any messages you post in community chat rooms, visible to other signed-in parents in that room.</li>
  <li><strong>Location data:</strong> if you enable location features, we use approximate location to help connect you with nearby parents. Location is not shared with other users beyond what's needed for this matching feature.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How We Use Your Information</h2>
<p>We use the information above solely to operate the app's core features: authenticating your account, personalizing your profile, enabling community conversations, and connecting you with nearby parents if you choose to use that feature. We do not sell your data to third parties.</p>

<h2>Data Storage and Security</h2>
<p>Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, and related services), which applies industry-standard security practices. Access to your profile data is restricted to your own account; community messages are visible to other members of the specific chat rooms you participate in.</p>

<h2>Children's Data</h2>
<p>NearbyCircle is designed for use <strong>by parents/guardians</strong>, not directly by children. Any information about a child (such as age) is provided by the parent and is used only to personalize the parent's experience within the app.</p>

<h2>Your Choices</h2>
<p>You can update or delete your profile information within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us using the information below.</p>

<h2>Changes to This Policy</h2>
<p>We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.</p>

<h2>Contact Us</h2>
<p>If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at: <strong>admysysapps@gmail.com</strong></p>
